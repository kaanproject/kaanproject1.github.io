How it works

Three steps from ignorance to action. Takes under 60 seconds.

01

Paste your URL

Drop in any website address. We crawl it, analyse assets, hosting, scripts, and data transfer in real time.

02

Get your score

Receive a carbon rating from A–F with grams of CO₂ per visit, yearly projections, and how you compare to similar sites.

03

Act on AI fixes

Our AI generates a prioritised fix list: switch hosts, compress assets, remove scripts — with estimated savings for each.
